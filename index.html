Our Story

Youth-powered change, rooted in compassion.

NayePankh — meaning "New Wings" in Hindi — was born during the COVID lockdown when a group of young students saw suffering around them and refused to look away. What started as a crisis response became one of India's largest student-led NGOs.

  • ✓ Founded and run by students, many still in school and college, proving change doesn't wait for credentials.
  • ✓ Expanded from emergency COVID relief to a full-fledged multi-city community upliftment program.
  • ✓ Featured in The Pioneer, Dainik Jagran, Hindustan Times and recognized by NITI Aayog.
  • ✓ 50% tax benefit on your donation under Section 80G — every rupee does more.
